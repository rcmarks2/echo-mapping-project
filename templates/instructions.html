How to Use Our Route Comparison Tool

Single Route

To generate a detailed diesel vs. EV comparison for one specific route, use the Single Route section on the homepage. Enter your start and end locations, your truck's MPG (optional), and the number of annual trips. Then click Generate Comparison.

Example of the Single Route section on the homepage.

Batch Upload

To analyze many routes at once, scroll to the Batch Route Upload section. Start by downloading the provided Excel template using the link labeled “Download here.”

Click "Download here" to get the Excel upload template.

Fill out the template with your route details: start city/state, end city/state, annual trips, and (optionally) MPG. Save your file, then upload it using the Upload Excel File field. Click Upload and Process Routes to view results for all routes.
