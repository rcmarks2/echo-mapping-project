How to Use Our Route Comparison Tool

Single Route

To generate a detailed diesel vs. EV comparison for a single route, use the Single Route section on the homepage. Enter your start and end locations, your truck’s MPG (optional), and the number of annual trips. Then click Generate Comparison to view side-by-side maps, cost, and emissions results.

Single Route section where you enter your route details.

Batch Upload

If you need to analyze multiple routes at once, scroll to the Batch Route Upload section on the homepage. Start by downloading the Excel template labeled “Download here.”

Click "Download here" to get the Excel upload template.

Open the Excel file, enter your start and end cities/states, MPG (optional), and annual trips for each route. Save the file and upload it using the Upload Excel File field. After clicking Upload and Process Routes, your results will be downloadable in Excel format.

Upload your completed template here to run multiple routes at once.
